In Vedic cosmology, the universe itself is a sonic event. Shabda Brahman — the Supreme as Sound — is the first principle.

Creation Through Sound — Shristi Krama
"Chatvaari vaak parimitaa padaani taani vidur Brahmanaa ye maneeshinah" — There are four measured quarters of speech. (Rig Veda 1.164.45)
Para (परा)
Transcendent unmanifest sound. Pure potential. Located at Muladhara. The silence that contains all sound.
Pashyanti (पश्यन्ती)
The "seeing" sound — where sound and meaning are undivided. Located at navel. Pure intuition of the poet/musician.
Madhyama (मध्यमा)
The "middle" sound — mental speech before articulation. Located at Anahata-heart. The inner voice.
Vaikhari (वैखरी)
The "spoken" sound — articulated physical speech. Located at Vishuddha-throat. Mantra in its gross form.
